
### Essential Reading

**Books:**
1. **"Network Forensics: Tracking Hackers through Cyberspace"** by Sherri Davidoff and Jonathan Ham
   - Comprehensive coverage of network forensics principles

2. **"Practical Packet Analysis"** by Chris Sanders
   - Hands-on guide to Wireshark and traffic analysis

3. **"Wireless Network Security: A Beginner's Guide"** by Tyler Wrightson
   - Foundational wireless security concepts

4. **"The Basics of Hacking and Penetration Testing"** by Patrick Engebretson
   - Ethical hacking methodologies applicable to wireless testing

- **EnCase Certified Examiner (EnCE)**

### Research Papers and White Papers

1. **"Weaknesses in the Key Scheduling Algorithm of RC4"** by Scott Fluhrer, Itsik Mantin, and Adi Shamir
   - Foundational paper on WEP vulnerabilities

2. **"A Practical Message Falsification Attack on WPA"** by Erik Tews and Martin Beck
   - Understanding WPA weaknesses

3. **"KRACK Attacks: Breaking WPA2"** by Mathy Vanhoef
   - Key Reinstallation Attacks research

4. **NIST Special Publications:**
   - SP 800-48: Wireless Network Security
   - SP 800-153: Guidelines for Securing Wireless Local Area Networks
